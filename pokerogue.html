PokeRogue

Embark on an endless adventure in this unique roguelike Pokemon card game. Build your deck, battle through procedurally generated challenges, and discover powerful synergies in every run!

About PokeRogue

PokeRogue combines the strategic depth of deck-building games with the endless replayability of roguelike adventures. Each run presents unique challenges and opportunities, as you collect Pokemon cards, discover powerful combinations, and battle through increasingly difficult encounters.

Game Features

Roguelike Elements

Every run is unique with procedurally generated encounters and rewards.

Strategic Depth

Build powerful decks and discover synergies between different Pokemon types.

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on building synergistic card combinations
  • Adapt your strategy based on available cards
  • Consider the risk vs. reward of each path
  • Learn from each run to improve future attempts

How to Play

1. Start each run with a basic deck of Pokemon cards.

2. Choose your path through randomly generated encounters.

3. Battle enemies and collect new cards to strengthen your deck.

4. Discover powerful combinations and unlock new Pokemon variants.
