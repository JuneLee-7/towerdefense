About Pokemon Tower Defense

Pokemon Tower Defense is the original game that started it all, combining the beloved Pokemon franchise with strategic tower defense gameplay. Released in 2011, this game quickly became a fan favorite, allowing players to use their favorite Pokemon in an entirely new way.

Game Features

Classic Pokemon

Use your favorite Generation 1 Pokemon to defend against waves of wild Pokemon.

Level Up System

Train and evolve your Pokemon as you progress through challenging levels.

Tips & Strategies

  • Position your strongest Pokemon at key choke points
  • Focus on evolving your core team early in the game
  • Use type advantages to maximize damage output
  • Don't forget to level up your Pokemon between stages

How to Play

1. Choose your starting Pokemon carefully - they will be crucial for early game success.

2. Place Pokemon strategically on the map to intercept incoming waves of enemies.

3. Collect candies from defeated Pokemon to level up your team.

4. Evolve your Pokemon when possible to unlock more powerful abilities.
